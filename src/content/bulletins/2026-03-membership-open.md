Membership signups are open! New members can [join here](/join/); returning members can renew through [My Account](/my-account/).

## Storage Spaces

Mooring, long-term RV, and trailered boat parking signup is no longer part of the membership form. We moved these to a separate process for a couple of reasons: our old system only allowed members to pay for a space at the time of membership signup, not later in the season; and we wanted members to have a chance to review the relevant policies and ask questions before bringing a boat or RV to the club.

If you held a space last season, you'll receive a separate email with a link to confirm your spot for 2026.

If you need one of these spaces and don't currently have one (or if you just have questions), get in touch with the site committee chair (Geoff Wright) or the harbor committee (TL or Dave J.). You can post in the `#harbor` or `#site` channel on Discord, or use the [Issues & Support form](/issues-and-support/).

## Classes

Class registration opens soon. Members get advance notice before we open signup to the public, so friends and family have a chance to sign up. Watch Discord and your email!
