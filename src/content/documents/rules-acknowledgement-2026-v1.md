# Alaska Sailing Club—Club Rules Acknowledgement

**Season: 2026.** I sign this document once per season.

The Rules of the Alaska Sailing Club are published on the club site as **Member
Expectations**. The full text of the current rules is presented to me with this
acknowledgement, and that is the version I am acknowledging.

**I have read the Rules of the Alaska Sailing Club, and I agree to follow them as a
condition of my membership.** In particular, I understand and agree that:

- **Life jackets are required** as the rules provide: at all times on club boats, for
  children 12 and under anywhere on club grounds or docks, for adult non-swimmers
  near the water, and for everyone aboard any boat in a club event.
- **The club runs on volunteers.** At least one work party a year is part of
  membership, and standing definitions (Member in Good Standing, Active
  Participating Member) depend on it.
- **I am responsible for my guests.** They are welcome, and their conduct, rule
  compliance, and required forms are mine to see to.
- **The rules can change.** The board may revise the rules; the published version on
  the club site is the version in force, and material changes are announced to
  members.
- **Breaking the rules has consequences,** up to termination of membership under
  Article III, Section 6 of the club bylaws.

If anything in this acknowledgement conflicts with the rules themselves, the
published rules govern.

## Signing

I agree that typing my legal name and clicking Sign creates a binding electronic
signature. For a family membership, each adult signs for themselves; a parent or
guardian confirms that the household's minors will follow the rules.
