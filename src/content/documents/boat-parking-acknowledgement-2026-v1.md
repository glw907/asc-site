# Alaska Sailing Club—Trailered Boat Parking Rules

**Season: 2026.** I sign this each season with my trailered boat space, alongside
the Dry Storage Agreement. My space sits on Matanuska-Susitna Borough land that the
Club manages, and my use of it is subject to the Borough instruments described in
that agreement.

**I have read these rules, and I agree to follow all of them:**

- **Active use.** My boat sails at least three times per season. This is space for
  boats that go in the water, not long-term storage.
- **Clean space.** I keep my space and the surrounding area clean.
- **Movable.** My boat and trailer remain movable, and the Club may move them when
  necessary for club activities.
- **Season end.** I vacate my space at the end of the season.

I understand that holding a space requires Active Participating Member status, and
that spaces are assigned by the Site Committee Chair in order of application.

## Signing

I agree that typing my legal name and clicking Sign creates a binding electronic
signature.
