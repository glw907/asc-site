---
title: "Transient RV Parking"
---

The Alaska Sailing Club welcomes members to bring RVs to the club for short stays. We have space for 3-4 RVs on Trailer Row, first-come, first-served. No reservation needed for stays up to four days.

## Before You Arrive

::::facts
:::fact[Maximum box length]
27'
:::
:::fact[Maximum stay]
4 days (longer stays require Site Committee Chair approval and must not conflict with an event weekend)
:::
:::fact[Power]
Provided at no charge (bring your own extension cord)
:::
:::fact[Water]
No potable water on site; lakewater sink available for dishes
:::
:::fact[Fire extinguisher]
Required on board
:::
:::fact[Mat-Su Borough compliance]
Your RV must be movable on short notice and removed within 72 hours if requested by the Mat-Su Borough. Failure to comply results in towing at your expense.
:::
::::

## When You Arrive

Park close to the next RV, but leave enough room for a motorized rig to pull out. Hug the right bank to keep the row center clear. If you're using a tow vehicle, disconnect it and move it to the regular parking area.

## During Your Stay

- No ground fires (fires are only allowed in the established waterfront fire pit)
- Keep the area around your RV clean and tidy; no trash
- No portable storage of gasoline, kerosene, or diesel fuel (propane in fit-for-purpose canisters is acceptable)
- Report any fuel release to the ASC board immediately via [Issues & Support](/issues-and-support/)
- No discharge of firearms; no explosives or fireworks along Trailer Row
- No gray water discharge anywhere on ASC grounds
- Lock your RV when not in use
- Put away sliders and awnings after each use

## When You Leave

Make sure the area around your RV is clean and tidy before you leave.

**The ASC accepts no liability for your RV or its contents.**

If you arrive and all spaces are full, you can park in the small boat launch area just outside the club for day use. For overnight stays, Big Lake North State Recreation Area is 6.4 miles from the club.
