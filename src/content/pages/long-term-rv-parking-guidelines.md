---
title: "Long-Term RV Parking Guidelines"
---

Long-term RV spaces are on Trailer Row. Most are occupied year-round by active members. Spaces are in high demand: waitlists commonly span multiple years.

Long-term parking is a seasonal commitment subject to Active Participating Member requirements. If you're planning a short visit with an RV (up to four days), see the [Transient RV Parking](/transient-rv-parking/) page instead. For current availability, see [Storage Waitlists](/waitlists/).

## Eligibility

To hold a long-term space, you must:

1. Be an **Active Participating Member** (as defined in ASC Rule XXIII)
2. Have dues paid in full by the specified seasonal deadline
3. Be in compliance with all ASC bylaws and rules
4. Have signed the Trailer Row Use Guidelines for the current season
5. Have your security deposit paid in full (new occupants must pay within 30 days of being offered a space)
6. Agree to follow all Matanuska-Susitna Borough land use permit conditions

## Applying and Renewing

### Renewing an existing space

Submit a signed copy of the Trailer Row Use Guidelines and your membership fee each season. If no application is received by the deadline, the board will assume you are vacating your space.

### Applying for a new space

To ask about availability, use the [Issues & Support form](/issues-and-support/?category=storage#report-an-issue) and select **Long-term RV parking**. You can also ask in `#site` on Discord. Submit a signed copy of the guidelines and your membership fee when a space is offered. Spaces are assigned in order of application; waitlist position carries forward season to season.

## Transfer of Ownership

Long-term parking spaces do not transfer with RV ownership. If you sell or replace your RV:

- **New owners** must submit a fresh application. If the waitlist is full and they are not next in line, the RV may need to be removed.
- **Existing space holders replacing their RV** must have the new vehicle inspected before placement.

## Rules for Occupancy

The following rules apply to all long-term spaces, based on the Mat-Su Borough Land Use Permit and ASC bylaws:

- RVs must be **actively used** (minimum six nights per season)
- RVs must be kept in livable condition at all times
- The surrounding area must be kept clean; no trash allowed
- Interior and exterior sanitary conditions must be maintained at all times
- **No portable storage of gasoline, kerosene, or diesel fuel** (propane in fit-for-purpose canisters is acceptable)
- Any fuel release must be reported to an ASC board member immediately
- No discharge of firearms; no explosives or fireworks along Trailer Row
- No dumping of gray water
- Regular trash removal required; pack out what you pack in
- Each RV must have a **fire extinguisher** on board at all times
- Disconnect electrical hookups at the end of each season
- Lock your RV securely when not in use
- Put away all sliders and awnings after each use
- RVs must remain movable on short notice
- RVs must be removed within 72 hours if requested by the Mat-Su Borough; failure to comply will result in towing at the member's expense
