---
title: "Issues & Support"
---

Report a problem, ask a billing question, submit a reimbursement request, or ask about storage availability. Boat problems route to `#fleet` on Discord, harbor issues to `#harbor`, and site issues to `#site`; billing, reimbursements, and storage inquiries go to the relevant committee by email.

For website problems or IT requests, use the [IT Request form](/it-request/) instead.

## What to Report Where

::::cards
:::card[Boat Issues]{icon="sailboat"}
Equipment damage, missing gear, maintenance. Routes to `#fleet` on Discord.
:::
:::card[Site Issues]{icon="wrench"}
Clubhouse, grounds, parking, utilities. Routes to `#site` on Discord.
:::
:::card[Harbor Issues]{icon="anchor"}
Docks, ramps, and moorings. Routes to `#harbor` on Discord.
:::
:::card[Storage Inquiry]{icon="package"}
Moorings, boat parking, rack storage, ask about availability. Routes to the Site Committee by email.
:::
:::card[Billing Question]{icon="credit-card"}
Dues, storage fees, payment questions. Routes to the Finance Committee by email.
:::
:::card[Reimbursement]{icon="clipboard-text"}
Club expense receipts (include photo or scan). Routes to the Finance Committee by email.
:::
::::

## Report an Issue

The issue-report form isn't wired up here yet. In the meantime, post in the Discord channel for
your issue (see below), or email `board@aksailingclub.org` for billing, reimbursement, or storage
questions.
:::

## Prefer Discord?

Club members can report issues directly in the Discord server:

- `#fleet`: Boat equipment and maintenance
- `#harbor`: Docks, ramps, and moorings
- `#site`: Clubhouse and grounds
