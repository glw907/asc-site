---
title: "Renewing Your Membership"
---

If you're past due, your benefits are on hold but your account stays open. Seasonal storage (moorings, RV, boat parking, rack) renews separately, so contact the Harbormaster for moorings or the Site Committee Chair for everything else through our [contact form](/contact/).

:::callout[Renewal Is Moving to the New Site]{tone="interim"}
Membership renewal is moving off MembershipWorks as part of this site's rebuild, and isn't wired
up here yet. In the meantime, email `board@aksailingclub.org` to renew, or ask in Discord.
:::

## Questions

**Changing membership type** (Individual to Family, or similar): renew first, then use [Issues & Support](/issues-and-support/?category=general#report-an-issue) to let us know what adjustment you need.

**Payment or billing issues:** use the [Issues & Support](/issues-and-support/?category=billing#report-an-issue) form.

**Forgot your password?** [Contact us](/contact/) and we'll help you get sorted out.
