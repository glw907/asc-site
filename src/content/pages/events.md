title: "Events"
---

The ASC season runs from Memorial Day through Labor Day, with regattas, Wet Wednesdays, classes, potlucks, and work parties throughout the summer. Most events are family-friendly weekends with camping, campfires, and plenty of time on the water.

The full calendar below reads straight from the club's own event records, so it's always current. Questions about a specific event? Check `#racing` and `#general` on [Discord](/discord-server/).
