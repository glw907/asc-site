# Alaska Sailing Club—Rack Storage Rules

**Season: 2027.** I sign this each season with my rack space, alongside the Dry
Storage Agreement.

**I have read these rules, and I agree to follow all of them:**

- **What racks are for.** Kayaks, paddleboards, canoes, and small sailboats
  (Lasers, Sunfish, Optimists, and similar). If I am unsure whether my watercraft
  qualifies, I ask the Site Committee Chair before storing it.
- **Active use.** What I store gets used during the sailing season.
- **Clean space.** I keep my rack space and the surrounding area clean.
- **Season end.** I remove everything at the end of the season.

I understand that holding a rack space requires Active Participating Member status.

## Signing

I agree that typing my legal name and clicking Sign creates a binding electronic
signature.
